Hi, I'm David Twomey

-Day-Vid- two-me- aka David2me

UX designer hopeful, wannabe software developer, with golfing that's woeful

Who am I?

I first got started in designing from a very young age, editing call of duty videos on Sony Vegas and designing graphics in adobe photo shop and after effects.

It took me 24 hours to render a minute long clips on the parents trusty old silver Dell, worth it.

My UI/UX journey

I studied Digital Humanities and I.T. in UCC graduating with a 2:1 degree in 2020

I completed 9 months of work placement at Poppulo, this is where I gained invaluable experience in UI/UX design from a very talented team. I have been continually improving my design thinking and coding since.

My Passion

My passion is to make the UI and UX of software, apps, and websites enjoyable and easier to use.

I'm continuously learning but please do take a look at some of my previous designs in the 'Work' section.
